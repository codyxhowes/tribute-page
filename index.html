Eddie Van Halen

One of the greatest guitar players of all time.

A time line of Eddie's life:

  • 1955 - Eddie Van Halen is born.
  • 1961 - Eddie begins piano lessons.
  • 1972 - Eddie forms the band Genesis.
  • 1976 - Van Halen is formed.
  • 1978 - Van Halens first album releases.
  • 1979 - Creates Frankenstrat guitar.
  • 1980 - Marries actress Valerie Bertinelli.
  • 1992 - Van Halen wins Grammy for Best Hard Rock Performance.
  • 1999 - Has hip replacement surgery.
  • 2000 - Starts treatment for tongue cancer.
  • 2007 - Enters rehab for alcohol and drug abuse. Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. Divorces Valerie Bertinelli.
  • 2009 - Marries actress Janie Liszewski. Releases his Wolfgang guitar model.
  • 2012 - Emergency surgery for diverculitis. Releases Van Halens final album A Different Kind of Truth.
  • 2020 - Eddie loses his battle with throat cancer.

"Rock stars come and go. Musicians play until they die."

-- Eddie Van Halen
